Most of the functionality in our app can be tested within the simulator. The only thing is if you would like to test taking a picture instead of choosing one for your profile picture, you will have to run it on a device. The app was designed for IOS 13.5. You may have to change the team/bundle identifier in order to run.
Since everything else is pretty straightforward and typical of a dating app, as the only side note, your quiz answers are used to order people displayed in the collectionView, with those at the top being the most "suitable" for you.

At the time of writing this readme, Xcode is claiming there is a compiler error in EditViewController, but there is none and it builds fine.
